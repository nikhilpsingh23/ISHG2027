Location

CIDCO Convention Centre is located in the heart of Navi Mumbai, offering excellent connectivity through road, rail, metro and air transport.

Car / Cab

Easily accessible via Palm Beach Road, Sion-Panvel Expressway and Mumbai-Pune Highway. Ample parking facilities are available at the venue.

Airport

Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj International Airport is approximately 25-30 km from the venue.

Upcoming Metro

Navi Mumbai Metro connectivity provides easy access to the convention centre.

Railway Station

Nerul Railway Station and Belapur Railway Station are located within a short driving distance.
